About SBA Calendar

Gregorian ⇄ SBA date conversion. The SBA year begins on the first New Moon after the March equinox, and SBA months follow the New Moon cycle — so a year has 12 or 13 lunar months. Dates are resolved with the same time-house rule the reading calculator uses, so a date never shows a different SBA month here than in a reading. Moon phases are astronomical facts computed for each date. This is a personal date tool only: no readings, interpretations, or transits.

Calculation settings

Sun and Moon signs are computed at 12:00 local time for the selected date, using the true-sky 13-sign boundaries and the same placement function as the chart calculator. To reproduce a specific chart's Sun/Moon sign exactly, set the same timezone and birth coordinates here — the Moon's apparent position is observer-dependent.
